About

Agricultive project is about providing information about the needs of Indian agriculture.If you are new to the Indian Agriculture department then you stumbled upon correct place as this website shows the face of Indian Agriculture.

It mainly focuses on 2 states namely :Telangana and AndhraPradesh.

It provides all the basic information regarding Fertilizers,pesticides to be used.

It also provides us with the soil and water conditions in each district.

This site provides information about the tropical weather conditions at specific places

As Telangana government declared some new districts so that the government can run easily

So we have a total of 31 districts in telangana.

It has a total of 13 districts, three in Uttarandhra, six in Coastal Andhra Region and four in Rayalaseema Region.

This website also provides basic toll-free numbers for farmers like kisan yojana.

In future we also intend to provide the statistics of wide variety of crops which can be used for survey reference and also for data engineers

Our main aim is to provide as much as information or knowledge to Indian farmers so losses occur must be less so that by 2050 there must be no shortage of food production in India for its highly increasing population.
