TURMERIC

Turmeric crop

Turmeric is a tropical herb and is grown in both tropics and subtropics. It will grow luxuriantly in shade if not too dense.

crop diseases

root rot
leaf bloch
leaf spot

Pesticides

Spray Carbendazim (1g/lit) or Mancozeb (2.5g/lit) +1ml Sandovit for 3-4 times.
Spray Dithane M-45 @3 g/litre of water (or) Bavistin @ 1 g/litre of water.

NOTE:ITS BETTER TO USE ORGANIC PESTICIDSES FOR BETTER FARMING PRACTICES AND YIELDS

  • Preventive Measures:

    Seed material should be selected from disease free areas.
    Seed material should be treated with Dithane M -45 @ 3g/litre of water or Bavistin @ 1 g/litre of water.
    Seed material should be dipped for 30 minutes in the fungicidal solution and should be shade-dried before sowing.

  • Water Conditions:

    Till sprouting is completed light irrigations at weekly intervals are essential since it is very susceptible to water logging conditions, and an excess water during rainy season, should be drained out other wise it may cause rhizome rot. Number of irrigations will depend upon the soil and climatic conditions.
